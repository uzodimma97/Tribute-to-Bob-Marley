Here's a time line of Bob Marleys life

  • 1945 - Born in Trechtown Kingston, Jamaica.
  • 1957 - Returned to Wilmanton Delaware USA, with his mother.
  • 1961 - Returned to Jamaica and started his music career.
  • 1963 - Formed the Wailers with Peter Tosh and Bunny Wailer
  • 1965 - Released debut single "Simmer Down"
  • 1970s - Rise to international fame with albums like "Catch a Fire"(1973) "Rastaman Vibration"(1976) and "Exodus"(1977).
  • 1977 - Diagnosed with acral lentiginous melanoma (skin cancer)
  • 1978 - Released iconic album.
  • 1981 - Passed away at age 36 in Miami, Florida.
  • 1984 - Posthumous album "Legend" released, becoming best-selling regea album of all time.
  • 1994 - Induted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
  • 2001 - Awarded the Grammy Lifetime Achievement award.
